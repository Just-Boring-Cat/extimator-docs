# Troubleshooting

Use this section for common issues and quick fixes.

## I did not receive a magic link

- Check spam or promotions
- Try requesting the link again after a minute
- Confirm the email address is correct

## I cannot log in

- Ensure you are approved for early access
- If you changed email, confirm the new email first

## My project did not save

- Check for the unsaved status indicator
- Click Save before switching projects

## Exports look wrong

- Save the project first
- Export again to refresh totals

## Still stuck

Use the Support and Feedback section to contact us.
